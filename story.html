☔ A Rainy Day in Our Town
Rain falls softly on the cobblestones. You pull your umbrella close, boots making little splashes.

Suddenly — a brown cat darts out from under the café awning and skids to a stop right in front of you. It’s Shahid. Of course it is.

He looks up with those big brown eyes, tail flicking despite the rain.

“I’ve been waiting for you, Assail. Answer four questions about yourself… and unlock something very special.”

He grins. “Ready?”
🌙 Question 1 of 4
Shahid sits up very straight, looking extremely official for a cat in the rain.

“Question one. What do you, Assail, love most in the whole world?”

He watches you with a very knowing look.
✨ That’s the one!
Shahid’s eyes light up!

“14 HOURS! Yes! I once watched you sleep through a thunderstorm, two phone calls, and a cat sitting on your face.”

He grins. “That was me, by the way. You didn’t even move.”

One down! Three to go. 🌸
🐾 Question 2 of 4
Shahid does a little spin on the wet cobblestones. He is very pleased with himself.

“Question two! What is your absolute favourite animal?”

He watches you very carefully. Almost suspiciously.
✨ Obviously! 🐾
“CAT!” Shahid does a tiny spin of joy and almost falls in a puddle.

“Obviously! And your FAVOURITE cat—” he gestures to himself very dramatically — “is standing right here in the rain for you. You have excellent taste, Assail.”

Halfway there! 💛
🍫 Question 3 of 4
You both duck under the café awning as the rain picks up. The smell of warm pastries drifts out.

Shahid hops onto the windowsill and clears his throat.

“Question three! What is Assail’s absolute favourite drink? The one that makes everything better?”
✨ She knows herself!
“CHOCOLATE MILK!” Shahid pounds the windowsill (very gently — he’s a polite cat).

“I KNEW IT. If love had a flavour, it would taste exactly like chocolate milk to you, wouldn’t it?”

He’s not wrong. He’s literally never wrong.

One more question! You are so close. 💛
🎂 Question 4 of 4
The rain softens to a gentle drizzle. Silver light filters through the clouds.

Shahid hops down and sits beside you on the café steps, tail curling neatly around his paws. He looks up at you very seriously.

“Last question. The most important one.”

A pause.

“When is your birthday, Assail?”
🎉 12th April!!
“12th APRIL!!”

Shahid leaps to his feet so fast he nearly falls off the step. His eyes go wide. His tail is going absolutely wild.

“YES! YES! I’ve been waiting for this moment!”

He throws open his arms — a cat, somehow, throwing open his arms.

“Are you ready? Because something very, very special is about to happen…”

The rain stops. The clouds part. And then —
🎂
Happy Birthday,
Assail 🌸
You answered every question perfectly —
because you are perfectly, wonderfully you.

Every laugh, every hug, every chocolate milk. 🍫

Today the whole universe celebrates you. 💛
